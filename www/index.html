About

Sitecore GO! allows for simple interactions with your Sitecore from your mobile device. The main goal is to allow editors and approvers to review workflows, states and items and commit actions on them based on the state definition.

This software requires pulicly accessible Sitecore instance and active editor/reviewr account. It also requires Web Item API Package installed on your Sitecore server.

This is beta software and we do not guarantee to be bug free. You may use this software free of charge, however we do not take any responsibility for its operation.
